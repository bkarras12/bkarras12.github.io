Executive Summary

  • Average bill: $19.79 (median $17.80).
  • Average tip: $3.00; average tip rate: 16.08%.
  • Tip dollars rise with bill size (corr ≈ 0.68).
  • Tip % tends to decline as bill size increases (corr ≈ -0.34).
  • Dinner and Sunday contexts show stronger tip dollars.

Recommendations

  • Track tip $ and tip % together.
  • Use a minimum bill threshold when highlighting tip % outliers.
  • Prioritize staffing/readiness for Sunday and dinner periods.
  • Collect richer operational fields for deeper causal analysis.

Appendix

tip_pct = tip ÷ total_bill × 100. Findings are descriptive associations, not causal claims.
